## Coverage Boundaries

- Scope covers only client registration address and actual residence address from section-14 rows `032-057`.
- Out of scope: contacts, documents, participants, employment, visual assessment, consents and action `Далее`.
- Mockups are used only to make manual UI steps executable and do not define business rules.
- `GAP-001` remains open as a non-blocking residual risk for incomplete or partially recognized DaData addresses.
- `GAP-002` remains open as a non-blocking residual risk for numeric/length invalid feedback: the source defines numeric classes, but does not define observable invalid feedback, filtering or transition blocking.
- `GAP-003` remains open as a blocking integration-scope gap for `kladr`: section `4.3` states model filling after DaData selection, but this UI baseline has no source-backed API/DB/log evidence artifact for manual execution.
