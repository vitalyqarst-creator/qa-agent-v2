## Coverage Boundaries

- Scope covers only employment, income and income confirmation rows `104-129` in section 14.
- Out of scope: personal data, passport, addresses, contacts, participants, visual assessment, consents, document questionnaire files outside income confirmation and generic `Далее` validation.
- Mockups are used only for interaction wording and do not define expected behavior.
- Residual risks remain visible: `GAP-001`, `GAP-002`, `GAP-004`, `GAP-005`, `GAP-006`, `GAP-007`, `GAP-008`, `GAP-009`.
